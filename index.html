About Us

TURBO, founded in 2016 is a lifestyle-food QSR positioned as a franchise serving healthy food with a creative approach to its customers at value pricing, fun packaging and atmosphere. Turbo is the answer to an increasing demand for healthy food, to be consumed regularly. We are committed to bringing fun, happiness and convenience to lives of our consumers by delivering food for thought.

  • Homemade sauces and bread
  • Fresh veggies and fruits
